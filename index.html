Welcome to ConnorApps.

Here at ConnorApps we aim to make applications that will help everyday people like you and I.We aim to provide different communities with different tools even if that is a guide for a game or utilities for the home.

Constant Updates

We aim to constantly update our apps and provide patches for bugs so that there are no launching/incompatibility problems and our apps work with most mainstream android devices.

Support

If you do have a problem with one of my apps then please do contact me at c.macdonald13@me.com and I will aim to fix the problem that you are having.

New Ideas

If you have any app ideas or update ideas for our apps which you would like to suggest then do email them to us at c.macdonald13@me.com with the subject ideas and we will take them into consideration. Credit will be given any ideas we use.

Apps made by the community,for the community

We are just everyday people like yourself and we want to help the community by making the apps for the community.We are not a corporation,we are a small company.We are not out to steal money from the users,but to provide them with a service.
